# IronTask - Terminal UI/UX Design

## Design Philosophy

- **Project-First** - Organize tasks by projects
- **Minimal & Clean** - Focus on content, reduce visual noise
- **Keyboard-First** - Vim-style navigation, no mouse required
- **Color-Coded** - Priority and projects at a glance
